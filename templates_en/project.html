European green pact

This project aims to evaluate the effectiveness of environmental policies adopted by the Member States of the European Union within the framework of the Paris Agreement and the European Green Pact.We use Machine Learning historical and technical data to project emission trends and generate evidence -based recommendations.

General objective

Analyze, through an interactive analysis platform, the evolution of CO₂ emissions in the EU and evaluate whether countries are on the way to fulfill their climatic commitments by 2030 and 2050.

Specific objectives
  • Display emission trends in an interactive dashboard.
  • Implement predictive models to estimate future emissions.
  • Evaluate the impact of climatic policies on key sectors: energy, transport and industry.
  • Formulate recommendations through simulations and benchmarking.
